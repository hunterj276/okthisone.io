A Star Wars Game
By Hunter Johnson

It is four years before the Battle Of Yavin. You are a tie pilot,
ace of the Empire. You patrol planets and dock on a Ton-Flak class
carrier. You are the commander.
One day, in orbit above Lothal, a planet with known rebel sympathisers,
Four A-wings and a CR90 Corvette, named the "Tantive 3", jump in.
Your Comms Specialist suggests to COMMUNICATE with them and force
them to state their business, while your most trusted pilot says that
they are obviously rebels and that you should order an ATTACK. What do
you do?
